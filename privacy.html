Jet Horizon — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Jet Horizon ("the Game") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

The Game collects the following data:

  • Leaderboard name and score — When you submit a score to the leaderboard, the display name you choose and your score are stored on our servers. This is optional.
  • Local game progress — Coins, skin purchases, level progress, and settings are stored locally on your device using browser/app storage. This data never leaves your device.

Advertising

The Game displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob). These networks may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or contextual ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage and interaction data

You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools to understand how players interact with the Game, including session duration, feature usage, and crash reports. This data is aggregated and anonymized.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

Data Retention

Leaderboard data is retained for as long as the service is active. Local game data persists on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your leaderboard data at any time by contacting us. Local data can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us at any time.
