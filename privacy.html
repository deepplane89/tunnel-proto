Jet Horizon — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Jet Horizon ("the Game") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Summary

Jet Horizon does not track you across apps or websites. We do not show third-party advertising. We do not sell your data. The Game collects only the limited information described below to operate the leaderboard and improve gameplay.

Information We Collect

  • Leaderboard name and score — When you submit a score, the display name you choose (any text you enter) and your score are stored on our servers so the leaderboard can be displayed to other players. Submitting a score is optional.
  • Gameplay diagnostics — Anonymous gameplay session data (such as score, run duration, in-game events, and whether you played on mobile or desktop) may be sent to our server to help us diagnose bugs and tune difficulty. This data is not linked to your identity.
  • IP address — When you contact our servers (for leaderboard submissions or diagnostics), your IP address is recorded automatically by the server. It is used to prevent spam and abuse (rate limiting). We do not use it to track you across apps or build advertising profiles.
  • Local game data — Coins, skin purchases, level progress, and settings are stored locally on your device using standard app storage. This data never leaves your device.

Tracking and Advertising

Jet Horizon does not contain third-party advertising and does not use any tracking SDKs (no AdMob, no Facebook SDK, no analytics SDKs that build user profiles). The Game does not request the App Tracking Transparency permission because it does not track you.

In-App Purchases

If in-app purchases are offered, they are processed entirely through the Apple App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

Data Retention

Leaderboard entries are retained for as long as the leaderboard service is active. Diagnostic session data is retained on a rolling basis (the most recent sessions only). Local game data persists on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your leaderboard entry at any time by contacting us. Local data can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
